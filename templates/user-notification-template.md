# User Notification Template

---

### Purpose

This template is designed to inform users about a security incident that may affect them, their data, or their ability to work. The goal is to provide clear, concise, and actionable information, manage expectations, and prevent further compromise without causing undue panic.

### Key Information Sections

#### 1. Notification Header

-   **Subject Line:** Clear and concise, indicating the nature of the communication (e.g., "Important Security Alert," "Action Required: Potential Phishing Attempt," "Service Outage Notification").
-   **Sender:** Clearly from the IT Security team or a designated incident response communication channel.

#### 2. Introduction & Incident Summary

-   **Opening Salutation:** Address the user appropriately (e.g., "Dear [User Name]," "Dear Team Member").
-   **Brief Overview:** State clearly and concisely what has happened or is happening. Avoid overly technical jargon.
    -   "We are writing to inform you about a recent security incident affecting [System/Service]."
    -   "Our security team has detected a phishing campaign targeting our organization."
    -   "A temporary service disruption is currently affecting [Service]."

#### 3. Impact on User

-   **Personal Impact:** Explain how the incident specifically affects the user or their work.
    -   "Your account may have been compromised."
    -   "You may have received a suspicious email."
    -   "You may experience intermittent access to [Service]."
    -   "There is no indication that your personal data has been affected at this time."
-   **Scope (if appropriate):** Mention if the impact is widespread or limited.

#### 4. Required User Actions

-   **Clear Instructions:** Provide specific, step-by-step actions the user needs to take. Use bullet points or numbered lists for clarity.
    -   "**Change your password immediately** at [link to password reset portal]."
    -   "If you clicked on a suspicious link or opened an attachment, please contact the Help Desk at [phone number/email] immediately."
    -   "Delete the suspicious email and do not click on any links or open attachments."
    -   "Report any unusual activity on your account."
    -   "Please restart your computer at your earliest convenience."
-   **Why the action is needed:** Briefly explain the rationale behind the requested action.

#### 5. What We Are Doing / What to Expect

-   **Our Actions:** Reassure users that the security team is actively managing the situation.
    -   "Our security team is actively working to resolve the issue."
    -   "We have implemented additional monitoring measures."
    -   "We are working to restore full service as quickly as possible."
-   **Expectations:** Inform users about what they can expect next.
    -   "We will provide another update by [Date/Time]."
    -   "You may receive a call from the security team for further investigation."
    -   "Normal service is expected to resume within [X] hours."

#### 6. Contact Information & Closing

-   **Support Contact:** Provide clear contact details for questions or to report further issues.
    -   "If you have any questions or concerns, please contact the Help Desk at [phone number] or email [email address]."
-   **Closing:** Professional and reassuring.
    -   "Thank you for your cooperation and understanding."
    -   "Sincerely, The IT Security Team."

### Example Template Structure (Markdown)

```markdown
# SUBJECT: IMPORTANT SECURITY ALERT: Action Required Regarding Your Account

**Date:** 2026-01-29
**From:** IT Security Team

---

Dear [User Name],

This is an important security notification from the IT Security Team.

We have detected unusual activity associated with your user account, indicating a potential compromise. Our security systems have identified an unauthorized login attempt from an unfamiliar location.

## **What this means for you:**

Your account, including access to [list critical systems, e.g., corporate email, internal applications], may have been accessed by an unauthorized party. As a precautionary measure, we have temporarily suspended access to your account to prevent further unauthorized activity.

## **Immediate Action Required:**

To restore your access and secure your account, please follow these steps **immediately**:

1.  **Reset Your Password:** Navigate to our official password reset portal at [Link to Password Reset Portal].
2.  **Report Suspicious Activity:** After resetting your password, please log in and review your recent activity. If you notice any unauthorized emails sent, files modified, or other suspicious actions, contact the Help Desk at [Help Desk Phone Number] or reply to this email.
3.  **Enable MFA (if not already enabled):** We strongly recommend enabling Multi-Factor Authentication for your account if you have not already done so. Instructions can be found here: [Link to MFA Setup Guide].

## **What we are doing:**

Our security team is actively investigating the scope and nature of this incident. We are monitoring our systems for any related activity and working to ensure the integrity of our environment. We will continue to update you as more information becomes available.

## **Need Assistance?**

If you have any questions or require assistance with the password reset process, please do not hesitate to contact the Help Desk at [Help Desk Phone Number] or [Help Desk Email Address].

Thank you for your prompt attention to this matter and your cooperation in maintaining our collective security.

Sincerely,

The IT Security Team
[Company Name]
```
